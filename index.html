About us

Yoga is our life! Literally. Yoga is our job, hobby but also a goal. The goal is to create a yoga school accessible to everyone. A school where you can practice at any time of the day and from anywhere in the world. We want to share our love for movement and emphasize how important it is in the modern world and in the life of every human being.
